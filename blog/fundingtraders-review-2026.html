The "Buy Once, Earn Twice" Model Explained

FundingTraders has a unique affiliate structure that rewards referrers on repeat purchases and funded payouts:

  • New buyer — earn 15% of the challenge fee
  • Repeat buyer — if the same trader buys another challenge, earn 10% again
  • Funded trader payout — when your referred trader receives a profit payout, earn 5% of that payout

This means a single referred trader who gets funded and trades profitably can generate passive recurring income every month. Ten funded traders could mean $500+ per month with no additional effort.

FundingTraders Rules to Know
